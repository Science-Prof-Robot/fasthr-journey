June’23 - Fasthr.AI Talent Screening Platform

ProductFasthr.AI - Unhackable personalized hiring assessments platform to close positions 5x faster
Critical Problems Addressed1. Duplicity of assessment questions, generic question banks and finite question options, making assessments ineffective. 2. Broken Proctoring leading to rampant cheating and malpractices by candidates 3. 60-70% candidate drop-off rate during assessments due to MCQ test format, binary evaluation approach and long duration of assessments.
Metrics Impacted1. Reduction in cost of hiring 2. Reduction in time to hire 3. Reduction in probability of mis-hire
Key customer pain points solved 1. Less interview bandwidth required from Technical Leaders 2. Total no. of interview rounds reduced, enabling recruiter meet their quotas more efficiently 3. Interviewers have access to deeper insights about candidate’s potential, improving interview experience for both interviewer and candidate 4. Improved candidate experience in hiring process, leads to higher joining ratio
Products in Pipeline1. Resume Evaluation Platform - AI evaluates 1000s of Resumes against the JD and each other to create detailed resume rankings based on parameters such as skills, location preference and professional background 2. Conversational Interview Intelligence Platform - Enhanced Interview experience empowering interviewers to ask deeper and specific questions to evaluate candidates and a detailed AI generated feedback report on candidate’s performance during the interview
